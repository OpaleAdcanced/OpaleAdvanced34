Opale 3

Opale 3 is a publishing chain for the production of academic training documents that can be used in class remotely and in a mixed setup. Opale provides a solution for editing, management and multi-format publication of academic training documents:

  • paper-based (Open Office and PDF formats)
  • Slideshow
  • Web site
  • SCORM 1.2 and 2004 modules that can be integrated into an LMS platform.
One application, two levels

This application provides two usage levels of the publishing chain:

  • Opale Starter, designed for trainers and teachers wishing to autonomously produce their training modules without needing any specific training.
  • Opale Advanced, designed for trainers and teachers with advanced production, content management, re-usability or indexation needs. This publishing chain is also designed for organizations, training centers and schools needing solutions for managing the production and the publishing of advanced academic training documents.
