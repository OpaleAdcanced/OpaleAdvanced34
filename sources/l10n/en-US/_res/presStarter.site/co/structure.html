Opale StarterA simplified publishing chain for "academic" pedagogical content
The module

An Opale Starter module is composed with divisions . They represent each part of your content.

The Division

A division can contain:

  • Content elements to expose information ;
  • Interactive quizzes with Single choice questions (SCQ) and Multiple choice questions (MCQ).

The type of information delivered in your course is explicitly specified by "pedagogical blocks" and the information can be grouped with as many titled parts as you like.

The resources

The content in Opale Starter is multimedia. You can insert:

  • text ;
  • images: jpg , gif or png ;
  • sounds and audio files: mp3 , opus ;
  • videos, animations: mp4 , webm , flv , and Flash ;
  • office documents: Word , Excel , PowerPoint , odp , odt , pdf , zip .
