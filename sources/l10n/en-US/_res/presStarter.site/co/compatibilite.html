From "Starter" simplicity to"Advanced" richness...

At any moment, you can switch your working environment and your contents to Opale Advanced. By doing so, you will benefit from additional features:

  • More quiz types and more powerful pedagogical items;
  • More publishing medium;
  • More possibilities to organize and recombine your workshop items.
How to swich to Opale "Advanced" ?

  1. Open the Wokshop > Workshop properties... menu;

  2. In the new window, click Edit properties;

  3. Choose Opale Advanced then click Save;

  4. Your workshop is now linked to the Advanced model and you can continue your work from where you left off!

Once you start using the features specific to the Advanced level, your content will non longer be compatible with the Starter level. Save your content before that if you want to keep a version that you can edit in Opale Starter.
