Opale StarterA simplified publishing chain for "academic" pedagogical content
Who is Opale Starter for?

  • For trainers and teachers wishing to easily create training modules on web and paper medium.

Why use Opale Starter ?

To...

  • Make online content for training sessions available for learners, to revise or add additional learning material;

  • Facilitate content updates thanks to single source mechanisms: a single source of content, multiple output medium;

  • Improve your content by adding multimedia resources: videos, animations, sounds and schemas;

  • Improve your content by adding quizzes automatically managed by Opale Starter : Single Choice Questions and Multiple Choice Questions.

And also to...

  • Bring a homogenous graphic identity to all your training documents;

  • Ensure content durability by saving it in an XML format that does not depend on the publishing tool.

Print publishing (OpenDocument)

If you encounter any problem when publishing paper documents or in web document with OpenDocument table or schema, check that you installed precisely the recommended version of OpenOffice or LibreOffice, and you might also need to configure it's path in some case, with the menu : "Application" -> "Preferences" -> "General" -> "External tools".
