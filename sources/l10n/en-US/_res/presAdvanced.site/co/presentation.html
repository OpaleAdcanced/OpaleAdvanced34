Opale AdvancedA rich publishing chain for creating pedagogical content
Who is Opale Advanced for?

  • For trainers and teachers wishing to create rich training modules for classroom, distance, or blended-learning.

  • To organizations, training center and training schools, seeking a tool to produce and publish pedagogical content.

Why use Opale Advanced?

To...

  • Edit a paper medium of a course and it's web version;

  • Create a slideshow;

  • Cater module content to the learners needs by simply recombining the contents without duplicating them;

  • Ease information maintenance thanks to a production process thought for output on different media (single source of content, multiple output media);

  • Improve your content by adding multimedia resources: videos, animations, sounds and schema ;

  • Improve your content by adding quizzes automatically managed by Opale: Single Choice Questions and Multiple Choice Questionss, Drag & drop, Fill-in the gaps etc;

  • Create SCORM 1.2 or SCORM 2004 compliant training content and publish them via a Learning management system (LMS);

  • Ensure content durability by saving it in an XML format that does not depend on the publishing tool;

  • Bring a homogenous graphic identity to all your training documents;

  • Promote distance training in your organization.

How to use Opale ?

The "Quick help" tab of this area provides some hints on the structure of Opale items, you can also read the user guide (fr).

OpenDocument publishing

If you encounter any problem when publishing paper documents or in web document with OpenDocument table or schema, check that you installed precisely the recommended version of OpenOffice or LibreOffice, and you might also need to configure it's path in some case, with the menu : "Application" -> "Preferences" -> "General" -> "External tools".

LaTeX

If you wish to use LaTeX mathematics formula, you need to install a LaTeX engine.

You can find detailed information on the page : aide à l'installation LaTeX (fr).
